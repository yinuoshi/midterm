Where do people enter and exit BART the most?

Founded in 1957, The Bay Area Rapid Transit (BART) serves 50 stations throughout the San Francisco Bay Area. Here are the five BART stations with the most traffic in October 2020, which are, not surprisingly, all in San Francisco.
